"Dan's Magic Shrooms", 2017, 360 x 480, Video

(Set volume to appropiate levels before interacting)

"Dan’s Magic Shrooms" is a video based on the comedic and idiotic premise of my character selling mushrooms. This video is a clear parody of “It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia.” As a big fan of the show I wanted to make my own parody of the series. Though not as professional nor good as the original product, I attempted to reach the same outcome of making people laugh. This was achieved by writing a competent script, overseeing the production, and editing the video to elevate its comedic premise. This was made with Adobe After Effects.
