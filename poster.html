Polkadot Poster | 2018 | Digital Art
Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator

This series of pictures is to show the process of making a fan made poster for a Polkadot Stingray concert. This collection contains four concept drawings and the final product poster. I wanted the poster to have a spooky design with a ghost in the middle and a disorienting design surrounding her. The final poster consists of the band’s female singer dressed as a Yurei, a Japanese ghost, just like in one of their music videos. I also included the band’s cat mascot at the bottom of the poster. The concept drawings were made in Adobe Photoshop and the final poster was made in Adobe Illustrator.
