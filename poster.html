Polkadot Poster | 2018 | 2550 x 3300 | Digital Art

Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator

This Polkadot Stingray concert poster is a fanmade product. This project was an internal test to see how creative I can be. For this poster, I explored diferrent concepts but decided to use a spooky theme. In the middle of the poster is a ghost surrounded by intersecting straight lines to cause a sense of distortion.
