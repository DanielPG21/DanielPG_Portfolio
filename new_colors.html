NEW COLORS

This set of photos are part of the recolor series, photos that were once black and white, turned to a palette of beautiful colors and patterns. For the first photo, the woman holding wheat, I decided to make it soft and bright in its tones and colors. This was done by painting in Adobe Photoshop with the use of fills and adjustments to said colors. For the second photo, the woman holding the mask, I placed patterns on the clothing and objects. This was done by liquefying and warping the patterns onto the original photo. I also colored her skin and face.
