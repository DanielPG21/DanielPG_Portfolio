RANDOM TRACKS

(Set volume to appropiate levels before interacting)

As some might know I love music, for this reason, I made a playlist for this project. I made a playlist composed of 30 songs from various genres. The playlist is quite diverse because I have no knowledge of what music someone has heard. With a large selection, the odds of the user experiencing a new song is higher. I want people to expand their catalogue of music and experience something new. I believe that having a broad catalogue of anything can lead to a greater understanding and outlook. The randomizer consists of two buttons, where the “Music Box” button will randomly choose a song. It will also show a set of photos from the Voyager’s Golden Record. I believe it's quite fitting for whomever encounters the record will also experience something new as well. Clicking the button will also play a 2 second audio clip of Daft Punk's "Robot Rock" song. The “Playlist” button will lead to a YouTube playlist with the featured songs.
