DANIEL PEREZ GOMEZ

Daniel Perez Gomez is an artist and a descendant of Mexican immigrants. The possibility of breaking the societal barrier placed amongst the oppressed seemed quite slim. Despite the circumstances, the yearning to achieve one’s true path has been too great and allowed the opportunity to follow his dreams of becoming an artist. The artist’s curious nature has led him to use every opportunity to learn something new and explore different mediums. With the education provided in SJSU, and an ever-growing catalog of artworks, Daniel is determined to create artworks that can create a deep impression to the people that view them and inspire others to follow their own artistic goals.

Daniel Perez Gomez is experienced with many Adobe Suite applications such as Adobe Audition, After Effects, Illustrator, Lightroom Classic, Photoshop, Premiere Pro, and Substance Painter. Other applications include the 3D software programs known as Blender and Maya. In terms of coding, Daniel is knowledgeable in HTML, CSS, three.js, and p5.js.
