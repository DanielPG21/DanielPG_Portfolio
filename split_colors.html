SPLIT COLORS

The similarities of these photos is the use of split toning. The first photo consisting of a parakeet was done via Adobe Photoshop. This was done by turning the image to black and white. After that I selected different colors for the shadows and highlights which led to the results seen on these images. I also made other minor adjustments. The second photo consists of three amiibos which was split toned on Adobe Lightroom. This also included the same process but in Adobe Lightroom.
