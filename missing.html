"Missing Person's Found Footage", 2023, 1024 x 768, Video

(Set volume to appropiate levels before interacting)

Missing Person's Found Footage is my attempt in covering the genre of horror by using the technique of found footage. I wanted to branch out from traditional methods of conveying horror such as jump scares, and music. In this artwork I wanted to use the method of analog horror in where the story is more obscure. This artwork was created with footage filmed by me, the audio is a heavily edited sound effect from the video game Subnautica. Programs used for this piece is Runway, After Effects, Audacity, and Audition.
