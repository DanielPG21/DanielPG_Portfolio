Explore New Music | 2021 | 600 x 640 | Code

p5js and Adobe Audition

(Set volume to appropiate levels before interacting)

Continuing with the theme of music, I made a randomizer playlist for this project. The playlist is composed of 30 songs from various genres. With a large selection of music, the odds of the user hearing a new song is quite high. This was done because I want to give people the opportunity to expand their catalog of music and experience something new. The randomizer consists of two buttons, where the “Music Box” button will randomly choose a song. It will also show a set of photos from the Voyager’s Golden Record. Clicking the button will also play a 2 second audio clip of Daft Punk's "Robot Rock" song. The “Playlist” button will lead to a YouTube playlist with the featured songs. This artwork was made with p5js and Adobe Audition.
